Unity Housing

We are a nonprofit dedicated to ending homelessness and housing insecurity in Johnson City, Tennessee. We strive to provide safe, affordable housing for those who need it most through medical respite.

Learn more about Medical Respite

Our Plan

We plan to address a critical gap in services by building a medical respite facility in Johnson City, Tennessee to house individuals experiencing homelessness who are too unwell for street life, but not sick enough for hospitalization.
